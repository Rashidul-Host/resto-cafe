About Us

Sree Vari Foods

A Journey of Sree Vari Foods started when the three musketeers decided to start a small unique Chaat & Tea Shop(TEACHAAT) near VKM, Salem area to pursue their dream. Their motto is to deliver quality products with zero compromise on health. Teachaat attracted more loyal customers, with word of mouth a leading silks shop invited them to start their business within their silks shop. Keeping with that quality and people's health they started a new venture called “Black Wings Resto Cafe”

At Black Wings Resto Cafe, we believe in the continuous relationship between fresh and delicious food and life's moments of enjoyment. Black wings is a place where food is designed in order to please all plates.
